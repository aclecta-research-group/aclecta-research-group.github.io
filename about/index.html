Who we are:

Aclecta

"Aclecta" is a play on "eclectic", the one word that describes us best. This mindset, finding inventive ways to express an idea, matches our skillset: eclectic, broad, and asymmetric. Our emphasis is on "top down" solutions built on rock solid foundations of hard work, thorough preparation, and years of skillful training.


Research

Our interest lies in solving the unsolved. We are committed to knowing, understanding, and overcoming the challenges our clients face. Their challenges ARE our challenges.


Group

No man is an island. We are a team of individuals whose personalities, intellects, skillsets, and drive us to excel as a unit. We are the digital mercenaries of intellect. To our clients we say, "Our team becomes a part of yours leaving you to focus on your clients while we focus on you."
